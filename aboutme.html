Biomedical Engineering:

After completing my first year in Honours Biomedical Engineering at the University of Waterloo, I have decided to embark on the path towards a specialization in Neuro-Engineering. With my experience in this program so far, I have obtained strong mathematical knowledge from calculus and linear algebra, worthwhile programming skills in C++ and C#, design management skills, the ability to develop promising prototypes, human factors knowledge, and organic and inorganic chemistry to a certain degree.

Taekwondo:

Through 14 years of experience I have learned to follow the Kyo Hoon Code of Honour. This includes the values of respect, modesty, integrity, courage, and indomitable spirit. Over the years I strived to obtain these traits and consequently, I acquired my 3rd degree black belt, 4 provincial sparring gold medals, and competed in 3 national competitions and won bronze at the US World Open. Simultaneously, I instructed to give back to the sport I am so passionate about.

Hobbies:
